WhichWay Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to WhichWay ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Images: When you upload photos to our App, we temporarily process these images to provide recommendations.
  • Preferences: Any text preferences you enter to help refine your recommendations.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect information about your mobile device, including model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our App, including features you use and time spent on the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Process and analyze the images you upload to generate recommendations
  • Respond to your requests and support needs
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends

Data Storage and Security

  • Image Storage: Images you upload are temporarily stored on our secure servers and in Cloudinary for processing. After processing, the images remain accessible only through secure URLs.
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: We use OpenAI's services to analyze images and generate recommendations.
  • Cloudinary: We use Cloudinary to store and process images.

Each of these services has their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing your personal information
  • Correcting inaccurate information
  • Deleting your information
  • Restricting or objecting to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@nostove.com.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@nostove.com.
