Frequently Asked Questions

What is WhichWay?

WhichWay is an AI-powered decision assistant that helps you choose between options. Simply take a photo of your options (like a menu or products), add your preferences, and get personalized recommendations.

Do I need to create an account to use WhichWay?

No, WhichWay doesn't require any account creation or sign-up. Just download the app and start using it immediately.

How does WhichWay analyze my photos?

WhichWay uses advanced AI technology to analyze the content of your photos and understand your preferences. Your images are processed securely and are not permanently stored.

What types of decisions can WhichWay help with?

WhichWay can help with a variety of decisions, including:

  • Choosing menu items at restaurants
  • Deciding between products while shopping
  • Selecting between multiple options for any decision
Is my data private?

Yes, WhichWay values your privacy. Images are processed securely and are not permanently stored. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.

Why does WhichWay need camera and photo library access?

WhichWay needs camera access to take photos of your options and photo library access to select existing images. These permissions are only used for providing recommendations and not for any other purpose.

How do I provide feedback or report an issue?

We welcome your feedback! Please email us at info@nostove.com with any suggestions, comments, or issues you encounter.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Make sure your app is updated to the latest version
  • Restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection

Image Upload Issues

  • Ensure you've granted camera and photo library permissions
  • Try using a different image
  • Make sure your internet connection is stable

Recommendations Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try uploading a clearer image
  • Add more specific preferences
